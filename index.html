Our Story

Sawdust and Sunflowers is the creative partnership of Michelle and David Leitko, a couple united by their dedication to handcrafted goods, sustainable materials, and the beauty found in everyday nature.

Michelle — Fiber Arts

Michelle is a fiber arts artist whose work spans a wide range of textile traditions. She weaves and hand-forms beautiful baskets, hooking and braiding rugs with an artisan's touch, and brings warmth to every space with her quilts and sewing creations. Her baskets are finished with a rich, hand-crafted black walnut stain she makes herself — a natural dye as unique as the pieces it adorns.

David — Woodworking

David works his magic in wood, crafting everything from beautifully turned ornaments and pens on the lathe to traditional hand-crafted woodwork pieces that blend function with artistry. Every piece reflects a deep respect for the material and the craft.

Together, they share a deep commitment to upcycling and repurposing materials, giving new life to what others might overlook. Whenever possible, they proudly source materials locally from Massachusetts, keeping their craft rooted in the community and landscape they love.
