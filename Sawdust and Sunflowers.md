Welcome to ***Sawdust and Sunflowers***, where craft, creativity, and a love of the handmade come together.

Born from two passionate makers, Sawdust and Sunflowers is the creative partnership of **Michelle and David Leitko**, a couple united by their dedication to handcrafted goods, sustainable materials, and the beauty found in everyday nature.  

**Michelle** is a fiber arts artist whose work spans a wide range of textile traditions. She weaves and hand-forms beautiful baskets, hooking and braiding rugs with an artisan's touch, and brings warmth to every space with her quilts and sewing creations. Her baskets are truly one-of-a-kind, finished with a rich, hand-crafted black walnut stain she makes herself — a natural dye as unique as the pieces it adorns.  

**David** works his magic in wood, crafting everything from beautifully turned ornaments and pens on the lathe to traditional hand-crafted woodwork pieces that blend function with artistry.  

Together, they share a deep commitment to **upcycling and repurposing materials**, giving new life to what others might overlook. Whenever possible, they proudly source their materials **locally from Massachusetts**, keeping their craft rooted in the community and landscape they love.  

Every piece from Sawdust and Sunflowers tells a story — *made by hand, made with heart.*
